## Why slow matters
Fast software can be great, but the pace of change can make it hard to build trust. A slower rhythm leaves room for review, repair, and documentation. It also creates space for teams to reason about consequences.

## Tiny practice
One small practice I like is a weekly cleanup pass. It is not a refactor sprint. It is a checklist of tiny repairs that keep the project breathable.

> "Speed is a tool, not a goal."
